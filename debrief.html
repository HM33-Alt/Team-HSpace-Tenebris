The Next Step

The exploration of Tenebris represents an exploration of ecosystems that thrive thorugh chemosynthesis. It features my personal interpretation of such a world derived from evidence-based research to create a new world that might not be far removed from plausible reality. While it may have begun as an understanding of how life can not just survive but thrive in a chemosynthetic environment, the project reflects what I have learnt about the importance of energy synthesis processes for any alien world (including ours), what is fundamental for living organisms, adaptation and most importantly, responsible exploration.
