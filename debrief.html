The Next Step

The exploration of Tenebris represents an exploration of an ecosystem that thrives because of chemosynthesis. It features my personal interpretation of such a world derived from evidence-based research to create a new world that might not be far from plausible reality. While it may have begun as a process to understand and hypothesize about chemosynthetic life, the project eventually expands to cover what I now consider to be the ingredients that help us understand how life survives and thrives: the importance of energy synthesis processes, availability of nutrients through geology, the ecosystem and the food chain.
